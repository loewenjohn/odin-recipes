Smoked Brisket

Juicy oak smoked beef brisket

In this recipe we will be seasoning and smoking a beef brisket from scratch. Juicy, Tender, and flavour packed would describe this brikset.

We will be smoking this brisket with a supreme blend pellets for about 12 hours wrapping about halfway through and then after it has rested for a while we'll slice it into hte most delicious slices of smokey goodness you have ever tried!

Ingredients

Rub
  • 2 tbsp garlic powder
  • 2 tbsp onion powder
  • 2 tbsp paprika
  • 2 tsp chile powder
  • 1/3 cup kosher salt
  • 1/3 cup coarse ground black pepper
Main
  • 1 (12-14 lb) whole packer brisket, trimmed
  • 1 cup beef tallow
  • aprox. 6 ft. of butcher paper
